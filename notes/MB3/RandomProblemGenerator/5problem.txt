Let \(x,y\) be real numbers such that $$\frac{x^2+y^2}{x^2-y^2}+\frac{x^2-y^2}{x^2+y^2}=8$$Evaluate$$\frac{x^8+y^8}{x^8-y^8}-\frac{x^8-y^8}{x^8+y^8}$$

Combining the left hand side into one fraction gives us \(\frac{x^4+y^4}{x^4-y^4}=4\). Thus, $$\frac{x^4+y^4}{x^4-y^4}+\frac{x^4-y^4}{x^4+y^4}=4.25$$Combining the left side into one fraction again gives us $$\frac{x^8+y^8}{x^8-y^8}=\frac{17}8$$So, our answer is \(\frac{17}8+\frac8{17}=\frac{353}{136}\)
